RelationshipGraph

Graph model capturing relationships between different entities (characters, locations, items, etc.).
Developed in C# and Unity for Trinity College Dublin dissertation project.

C#/Unity

The RelationshipGraph is a graph implementation that can be used to capture and store relationships between different entities. The graph makes use of the idea of entities having "indirect edges" that allows entities to be aware of the relationships between other entities. For example, consider two entities Alice and Bob that have a "friendly" relationship; the RelationshipGraph allows a third entity, Charlie to be aware of this relationship.

The RelationshipGraph is generic enough to support such abstract concepts as group dynamics or associations, and recalling past relationships. Check out the project on Github or take a look at some of the demos.

Spectrinium
C#/Unity3D

Group project at Trinity College Dublin where we developed a simple game where you could switch between wavelengths of light. Depending on what wavelength you were on, you would encounter enemies that could only attack you if you were on the same wavelength as them.
The project was geared towards learning more about SCRUM and Agile development in a group as opposed to working on your own individual projects.
